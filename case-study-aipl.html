About

With over two decades in the real estate industry and a well-diversified portfolio of large scale projects, AIPL has emerged to be a respected name in Gurugram, committed to quality in service.

Overview

Large corporate offices come with challenges with regard to high visitor and employee volumes. AIPL Real Estate , wanted a solution that provided approval based access to specific office zones for both staff and visitors, a reduction in queues for visitors at the registration desk and a seamless visiting experience within their campuses.

Safety of their employees was also a focal concern, along with monitoring regularity and timeliness of staff.

What We Did

Hipla stitched together Visitor Management, Indoor Navigation, Pantry Management, Access Control and Energy Management systems into a singular mobile application that would boost efficiency, rationalise costs and provide intuitive intelligence as per staff and visitor trends.

Visitor Management System - Monitoring, tracking, visitor records and history

Indoor Navigation – Intelligently guide visitors to their meeting room and disallow access to unauthorized parts of the office .

Pantry Management System – Viewing availability of inventory, inventory management and placing refreshment orders right from meeting rooms

Access Control System - Authenticates the identity of employees and guests based on information fed into the system.

Energy Management System - Automates the monitoring, controlling, and conserving of electricity consumption in the office or building.
